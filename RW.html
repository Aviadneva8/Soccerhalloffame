1. Stanley Matthews

Goals 91/Appereances 837

Major Clubs: Stoke City, Blackpool

Sir Stanley Matthews, "The Wizard of The Dribble", is one of the great legends in history of British football and the greatest right-winger of all time. He is the only player to have been knighted while still playing, as well as being the first ever Ballon d'Or winner. Matthews was famous as one of the most skillful and best dribblers and one of the most accurate crossers ever. He kept fit enough to play at the top level until he was 55 years old, the oldest player ever to play in England's top football division.
