Midfield

Midfielders are the central pivot of the team.
Midfielders are generally positioned on the field between their team's defenders and forwards.

The Midfirlders are divided to 3 positions:
CDM (Defensive Midfielders), which their aim is the shatle, cover, tackle and sometimes dictate from deep. They're physically strong and good in the air. Moreover they're ought to have good defensive skills.

CM (Central Midfielders), which are 50/50 multitasking midfinders and even roaming playmaykers. Probably the toughest (aerobically speaking) position to play. They usualy possess good tackling, capable of passing from all ranges and are very good shooting from distance. Their most knwon trait is their stamina.

CAM (Attacking Midfielders), which are advanced playmaykers and ther're the focal point of creating. Usualy more technical and more visionative than other players thry're olso known for their passing and for assinting goals.
