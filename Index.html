Soccer's Hall of Fame

I'm Aviad Niewodowski, a self-proclaimed expert of the history of Soccer.

Before declaring that you're a Soccer expert, you need to explore this website!
This site is a ranking of the "best of the best". A ranking of the best players in every position throughout the history of "The Beautiful Game".
This Ranking is based on my own opinion and knowledge.

Before jumping in and starting to read there're some points you sould know:

  • For further information you may click on the footballer's name. The name is essentially a link to the footballer's Wikipedia page.

  • The footballers' positions are designated by me. If you're browsing on a this website and a footballer you expect finding isn't there, it could be that I designated him in a different position.
  • We don't all see "Eye to Eye". If you expect finding a player in the website and he isn't there, it could be that I don't rank him as highly as you do.

  • By hovering on the all of the players' images, the image will switch to the player's corresponding FIFA ULT Card.
    • More on that
  • Current players are designated by a blue & gold background on their FIFA ULT card. Past players are designated by a white background.

  • The players' major clubs are decided by me and based on my knowledge, they're ordered based on the player's career path.
